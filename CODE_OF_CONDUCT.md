# Code of conduct

Agent Anthill follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

We commit to a harassment-free, respectful community regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Examples of positive behavior include empathy, constructive feedback, respect for differing viewpoints, accepting responsibility, and focusing on what benefits the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized attention, insults, threats, doxxing, and sustained disruption.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communication that violate these standards. Once the repository is published, enforcement reports should use a private maintainer contact or GitHub's private reporting mechanisms, not a public issue.

Enforcement follows the Contributor Covenant Community Impact Guidelines: correction, warning, temporary ban, and permanent ban according to impact and pattern.
